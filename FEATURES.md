## 4. Credit & Accounting

- **Credit limit per client** - Set and manage credit limits for each client account
- **Real-time credit availability** - Monitor available credit in real-time
- **Auto invoice generation** - Automatically generate invoices based on transactions
- **Client ledger** - Complete transaction history and ledger for each client
- **Outstanding balance** - Track and manage outstanding balances
- **Payment terms (7/15/30 days)** - Flexible payment terms configuration
- **Aging reports** - Generate aging reports for accounts receivable
- **Returns & adjustments** - Handle returns and account adjustments
